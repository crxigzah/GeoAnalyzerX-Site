to master GeoGuessr

Intelligent Scene Reading

GeoAnalyzerX analyses vegetation type, soil colour, road markings, architecture, signage and terrain to identify exactly where in the world you are, far beyond what simple clue-lists can do.

Post-Round Teaching

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Can I cancel my Pro subscription?+
Yes, anytime. Pro is billed monthly through Stripe. You can cancel through the Stripe customer portal (accessible from Account Settings → Payment Methods) and your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period. No questions asked.
Does GeoAnalyzerX work offline?+
AI analysis, teaching popups, and GeoX chat all require an active internet connection. F7 scene capture requires a connection to upload to the community library.
Does GeoAnalyzerX work in all game modes?+
GeoAnalyzerX works in standard single player games and training modes. It must never be used in competitive multiplayer, ranked duels, or any game mode where you play against other real players. It is a training tool only.
How accurate is the location analysis?+
When you are in an active game round, GeoAnalyzerX uses the exact GPS coordinates from GeoGuessr and confirms the precise location instantly. Outside of a game, the AI analyses visual clues to estimate location with high accuracy for well-covered regions.
What is the community scene library?+
Every time any user presses F7 during a game, the scene is uploaded to a shared cloud library tagged by country and region. When a training guide fires, it pulls reference images from this library to compare your scene with real examples from the correct location. The more players use F7, the better the library gets for everyone.
Can I use GeoAnalyzerX on mobile?+
GeoAnalyzerX is a browser extension for Opera and Chrome on desktop. It does not currently support mobile browsers. We recommend playing GeoGuessr on desktop for the best training experience.
What happens to my data?+
Scene images uploaded via F7 are stored in our community library and may be seen by other users as reference images. Your account details (email, username) are stored securely and never sold or shared. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?+
You can cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal. Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period. Contact support@geoanalyzerx.com if you need any help with your subscription.
