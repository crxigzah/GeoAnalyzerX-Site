in minutes

Features

Everything you need
to master the world

A complete training system that learns alongside you and adapts to your weaknesses.

🧠

Intelligent Scene Reading

5kable analyses vegetation type, soil colour, road markings, architecture, signage and terrain to identify exactly where in the world you are, far beyond what simple clue-lists can do.

📖

Post-Round Teaching

After a wrong guess, a detailed lesson appears with your scene, a reference photo from the correct region, a dead giveaway clue, and 3 visual teaching points tailored to exactly what you missed.

🗺️

Community Scene Library

Every F7 capture is uploaded to a shared cloud database organised by country and region. Teaching popups pull the most relevant reference scenes from thousands of community contributions.

💬

Live Chat with 5K

Ask follow-up questions mid-lesson. "Why does inland Brazil look like this?" or "What's the giveaway for rural Finland?" 5K gives specific, location-aware answers.

📍

Region-Aware Comparisons

5kable distinguishes between northwest and southeast Australia, coastal and inland Brazil. Reference photos come from the exact matching region, not just the country.

⚡

F7 Instant Capture

One keypress uploads your current Street View scene to the community cloud library, correctly categorised by country and region. No forms, no clicks, no interruption.

🌍

114 Countries Supported

Works across all 114 supported countries. Any map, any round, any location worldwide.

🎯

Dead Giveaway Training

Every lesson highlights the single most identifiable visual feature the one thing you should have seen first. Over hundreds of rounds, you build instinctive pattern recognition.

🔬

Accurate Border Detection

Uses real geographic border coordinates to correctly classify locations in tricky boundary areas northern Victoria, southern Queensland, border regions in Europe and South America.

Rules & Acceptable Use

5kable is a singleplayer training tool only. It must never be used during competitive or multiplayer rounds against other players.

⚠️ IMPORTANT READ BEFORE USING

🚫 This tool is strictly for practice and training only

  • 5kable must NEVER be used during online multiplayer matches, ranked duels, competitive challenges or any game mode where you are playing against other real players.
  • Using AI assistance against other players is cheating and strictly prohibited. It violates the Terms of Service of competitive map-guessing games, which prohibit the use of third-party software to gain an unfair advantage.
  • Accounts found using 5kable in competitive play will have their 5kable access permanently revoked without refund.
  • 5kable is designed exclusively for solo practice maps, single-player exploration, and offline training sessions to improve your genuine geographic knowledge.
  • The goal of this tool is to make you genuinely better at geography, not to replace skill with automation. Use it to learn, not to cheat.
By using 5kable you agree to these rules. Violation detection may involve automated monitoring. If you believe an account action was taken in error, contact us on Discord.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Can I cancel my Pro subscription?+
Yes, anytime. Pro is billed monthly through Stripe. You can cancel through the Stripe customer portal (accessible from Account Settings → Payment Methods) and your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period. No questions asked.
Does 5kable work offline?+
AI analysis, teaching popups, and 5K chat all require an active internet connection. F7 scene capture requires a connection to upload to the community library.
Does 5kable work in all game modes?+
5kable works in standard single player games and training modes. It must never be used in competitive multiplayer, ranked duels, or any game mode where you play against other real players. It is a training tool only.
How accurate is the location analysis?+
In singleplayer practice rounds, 5kable reads the round's GPS coordinates and confirms the precise location instantly for review after you answer. Outside of a game, the AI analyses visual clues to estimate location with high accuracy for well-covered regions. 5kable must never be used during competitive or multiplayer rounds.
What is the community scene library?+
Every time any user presses F7 during a game, the scene is uploaded to a shared cloud library tagged by country and region. When a training guide fires, it pulls reference images from this library to compare your scene with real examples from the correct location. The more players use F7, the better the library gets for everyone.
Can I use 5kable on mobile?+
5kable is a browser extension for Opera and Chrome on desktop. It does not currently support mobile browsers. We recommend training on desktop for the best experience.
What happens to my data?+
Scene images uploaded via F7 are stored in our community library and may be seen by other users as reference images. Your account details (email, username) are stored securely and never sold or shared. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?+
You can cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal. Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period. Contact support@geoanalyzerx.com if you need any help with your subscription.
