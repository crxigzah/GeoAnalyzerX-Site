How It Works

From install to improvement
in minutes

1

Install the Script

Add Tampermonkey to your browser, install GeoAnalyzerX and enter your Anthropic API key. Takes 2 minutes.

2

Play Normally

Open GeoGuessr and play as you normally would. Press F7 to capture the scene before you guess.

3

Get a Lesson

After a wrong guess, a teaching popup explains exactly what you missed — dead giveaway, visual clues, reference photo.

4

Build Your Library

Every correct guess saves to your personal scene library by country and region. Comparisons get smarter as it grows.

Features

Everything you need to dominate

A complete training system that learns alongside you and adapts to your weaknesses.

🧠

🧠 Intelligent Scene Reading

Claude AI analyses vegetation type, soil colour, road markings, architecture, signage and terrain to identify where in the world you are — far beyond what simple clue-lists can do.

📖

📖 Post-Round Teaching

After a wrong guess, a detailed lesson appears with your scene, a reference photo from the correct region, a dead giveaway clue, and 3 visual teaching points tailored to exactly what you missed.

🗺️

🗺️ Personal Knowledge Base

Your scene library grows automatically as you play — organised by country and region. Over time, comparisons become hyperlocal and incredibly accurate.

💬

💬 Live Chat with GeoX

Ask follow-up questions mid-lesson. "Why does inland Brazil look like this?" or "What's the giveaway for rural Finland?" — GeoX gives specific, location-aware answers.

📍

📍 Region-Aware Comparisons

GeoAnalyzerX distinguishes between northwest Queensland and southeast Queensland, coastal Brazil and inland Brazil. Reference photos come from the matching region — not just the country.

⚡

⚡ F7 Instant Capture

One keypress saves your current Street View scene to the library, correctly categorised by country and region. No forms, no clicks, no interruption.

🌍

🌍 195 Countries Supported

Works everywhere GeoGuessr goes — all 195 countries, every game mode including World, Country Streaks, Duels and Custom Maps. Any round, any location.

🎯

🎯 Dead Giveaway Training

Every lesson highlights the single most identifiable visual feature — the one thing you should have seen first. Over hundreds of rounds, you build instinctive pattern recognition.

🔬

🔬 Accurate Border Detection

Uses real geographic border coordinates to correctly classify locations in tricky boundary areas — northern Victoria, southern Queensland, border regions in Europe and South America.

Rules & Acceptable Use

GeoAnalyzerX is a training tool. Using it during competitive play is cheating and violates GeoGuessr's Terms of Service.

⚠️ IMPORTANT — READ BEFORE USING

🚫 This tool is strictly for practice and training only

  • GeoAnalyzerX must NEVER be used during online multiplayer matches, ranked duels, competitive challenges or any game mode where you are playing against other real players.
  • Using AI assistance against other players is cheating. It violates GeoGuessr's Terms of Service (Section 4 — Prohibited Conduct) which prohibits the use of third-party software to gain an unfair advantage.
  • Accounts found using GeoAnalyzerX in competitive play will have their GeoAnalyzerX access permanently revoked without refund.
  • GeoAnalyzerX is designed exclusively for solo practice maps, single-player exploration, and offline training sessions to improve your genuine geographic knowledge.
  • The goal of this tool is to make you genuinely better at GeoGuessr — not to replace skill with automation. Use it to learn, not to cheat.
